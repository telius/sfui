# sfui

Modular and dynamic additions for the World of Warcraft interface with an emphasis on being lightweight considering the features it provides. 

SFUI is built for players who want a powerful, high-fidelity HUD without the configuration fatigue typical of complex frameworks. It replaces the need for a library of different addons by providing a unified, high-performance solution for tracking, automation, and UI enhancements.

### Tracking Manager
The Tracking Manager serves as the primary control center for your interface. It allows for effortless configuration of auras, cooldowns, and custom resource bars with live visual previews, ensuring your HUD is perfectly positioned for your playstyle.

![Tracking Manager](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/telius/sfui/main/.previews/trackingmanager.png)

### Tracked Bars
The tracking system integrates directly with your existing unit frames. By intelligently stacking cooldowns and ability procs above your health or resource bars, SFUI maintains combat awareness while keeping the screen clear of unnecessary clutter.

![Tracked Bars](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/telius/sfui/main/.previews/trackedbars.png)

### Master's Hammer Repair
SFUI features a smart repair system designed for professional crafters. When a character carries a Master's Hammer, the addon automatically detects damaged gear and provides a context-sensitive repair popup. It prioritizes weapons and plate armor first, allowing you to cycle through your repairs with simple clicks or a single keybind without wasting durability on less critical items.

![Master's Hammer Repair](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/telius/sfui/main/.previews/mastersrepair.png)

### Item and Currency Tracking
Customizable tracking for your most important session goals is handled through a sleek item tracker. Whether you are monitoring profession materials, specific currencies, or high-value consumable cooldowns, the tracker provides at-a-glance information that is easily managed with simple mouse clicks.

![Item Tracker](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/telius/sfui/main/.previews/itemtracker.png)

### Skyriding HUD
Modern flight systems deserve a modern interface. SFUI enhances the Skyriding experience with a streamlined HUD that prioritizes speed and vigor visibility while removing redundant Blizzard elements.

![Skyriding HUD](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/telius/sfui/main/.previews/dragonflying.png)

### Performance and Integration
The addon is optimized for the modern game engine, utilizing throttled API cycles and frame pooling to ensure zero impact on framerates during intensive combat. It supports Masque for consistent button skinning and includes various quality-of-life automations like the removal of merchant clutter and automatic grey-item liquidation.
